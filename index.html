What is Uganda Nurse Career Resourcing and what do they do ?

Uganda Nurse Career Resourcing is a Nursing Consultancy firm engaged in online nursing training courses and advices on recruiting countries and organisations of nurses allover the world.

We endeavour to be always on the side of nurses through offering holistic support for them to achieve their dreams of working abroad leveraging on several years of combined experience doing exactly this. Find advise nurses on how to find nursing opportinities in developed nations like United States of America, The United Kingdom, Australia, Canada and some more others.

On additional to this we provide online classes on how to utilize that chance while working abroad, we also advise on what you will expect to find abroad as a nurse, how life works there and how to over come difficults if you found any.

About Uganda Nurse Career Resourcing

It's based in Uganda and managed by a team of overseas Nurses whose mission is to offer support to Nurses relocating to overseas countries.

Advises nurses on how to find local and international job opportunities and also accessing some for chanced lucky nurses if they come in times where the job is available on the table.

Provides online classes or lessons for nurses on what to do when they have reached abroad how to stay so that life can be smooth for them.

Also provides teaches nurses on how to pass there exams and also interviews that are likely to be given to them to access the job. These could include, IECTS, OET, CBT, NMC and account opening.
